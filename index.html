Our Story

Khan Constructions has been a trusted name in civil construction since 2010. Founded by Shahil Khan, we've grown from a small local contractor to a respected firm handling multi-crore projects across North India.

Our Materials

Dholpur Stone

Dholpur Stone is a natural sandstone known for its warm pink-beige color and durability. It's widely used in flooring, cladding, and paving applications due to its weather-resistant properties and aesthetic appeal.

Characteristic Details
Color Pink-beige with natural variations
Common Uses Flooring, cladding, paving, wall facades
Available Sizes 12x12", 16x16", 24x24", custom sizes
Thickness 15mm, 20mm, 30mm
Finish Natural, honed, polished, brushed
